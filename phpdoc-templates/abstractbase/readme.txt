INSTALLATION:

1. Put this folder in phpDocumentor/Converters/HTML/frames/templates/


2. Copy HTMLframesConverter.inc

  - Make a symlink: to templates/abstractbase/HTMLframesConverter.inc in phpDocumentor/Converters/HTML/frames/
    $ cd phpDocumentor/Converters/HTML/frames
    $ ln -s templates/abstractbase/HTMLframesConverter.inc

  - Or you can simply copy "HTMLframesConverter.inc" to phpDocumentor/Converters/HTML/frames/HTMLframesConverter.inc
